### Start with repetitive daily tasks

The fastest automation wins come from tasks you already repeat: status updates, follow-ups, data entry, and meeting prep.

- Identify three recurring tasks you perform every day.
- Map triggers, inputs, and expected outputs for each one.
- Automate one workflow before expanding to the next.

### Build reliable routines

Consistency matters more than complexity. Simple automations that run every day create compounding time savings.

- Use calendar and inbox triggers for predictable work.
- Add alerts when automation fails or needs review.
- Review run history weekly to refine steps.

### Conclusion

Automating your day is less about doing everything with AI and more about removing friction from the work that slows you down.
