## Research Mode section

> Place as a feature callout section on the pricing page, between the plan

### What is Research Mode?

Research Mode is Nexus AI's deep research engine. Unlike a single AI reply,
Research Mode plans a multi-step research strategy, searches academic
databases and the web, reads sources, and produces a fully cited report —
with every claim linked to the source it came from.

**How it works:**

1. **You ask a research question.** Optionally set source preferences —
   academic only, web and academic, news, or your own uploaded library.
2. **Nexus AI builds a research plan.** You can review and edit it before
   it runs.
3. **Multi-step search.** Nexus AI searches Semantic Scholar, PubMed, arXiv,
   CrossRef, live web, and your Folders simultaneously.
4. **Source reading and synthesis.** Every claim in the output is tied to a
   specific source, page and passage.
5. **Cited report.** You receive a structured report with an executive
   summary, thematic findings, methodology note, limitations and a full
   reference list in your chosen citation style.
6. **Refine.** Ask follow-up questions, drill into a finding, or ask Nexus AI
   to search for counter-evidence.

**Research Mode vs the competition:**

| | ChatGPT Deep Research | Perplexity | Nexus AI Research Mode |
|---|---|---|---|
| Multi-step research plan | Auto only | Partially visible | User-editable before it runs |
| Academic database search | Web only | Partial | Semantic Scholar, PubMed, arXiv, CrossRef |
| Your uploaded files | No | No | Yes — searches your Folders too |
| Citation styles | Basic | Basic | 10,000+ styles, inline + reference list |
| Edit in Canvas | No | No | Yes |
| Peer review check | No | No | Yes — via Nexus AI Academic |
| Visual output (charts, slides) | No | No | Yes — via Creative Studio |

**Available on:** Premium (20 queries/month), Ultimate (unlimited),
Teams, Business, Education and Enterprise.

## Nexus AI Academic section

### The academic workspace that does everything

Nexus AI Academic is a purpose-built research and writing workspace for
students, researchers, PhD candidates and academic teams. Write with AI
grounded in your sources, manage your library, generate citations, check
originality, and simulate peer review — all in one place.

**Core capabilities:**

- **AI writing with inline suggestions** grounded in your uploaded sources.
  Every suggestion is traceable to a specific page and paragraph.
- **200M+ paper academic search** — Semantic Scholar, PubMed, arXiv,
  CrossRef and more.
- **10,000+ citation styles** — APA 7th, MLA, Chicago, Harvard, IEEE,
  Vancouver, journal-specific formats and more. One-click insertion.
  Auto-generates your reference list. Export to .bib and .ris.
- **Plagiarism checker** — checks your writing against academic databases
  and the web. Returns an originality score and matched sources.
- **Peer review simulation** — get structured feedback on unverified claims,
  unsupported arguments, contradictions with your sources, and logic issues
  before you submit.
- **Literature review generation** — define a topic, select sources, and
  Nexus AI writes a structured literature review with thematic sections and
  a research gap summary.
- **Thesis statement generator** — generates candidate thesis statements
  from your topic and sources, with supporting evidence links.
- **Essay outline generator** — structured outline with the sources mapped
  to each section.
- **Paraphrasing and expansion** — rewrite at sentence or paragraph level,
  or expand using sources from your library.
- **Summariser** — summarise a single PDF, a collection, or a passage, with
  citations on every key point.
- **Zotero and Mendeley import** — bring your existing library in instantly.
- **Export to Word, PDF, LaTeX, HTML, .bib, .ris.**
- **Research Mode integration** — run deep multi-step research from inside
  your Academic workspace.
- **Creative Studio integration** — generate figures, charts, visual
  abstracts and slide decks without leaving Nexus AI.

**Available on:** Premium (full access), Ultimate (advanced),
Teams, Business, Education and Enterprise.

## FAQ section

> Render as an accordion. Place below the comparison table on the main pricing page.

**Q: Which plans have annual billing?**
Annual billing is available on Premium ($16/month, $192/year) and Ultimate
($80/month, $960/year). Teams and Business plans are billed monthly per seat
only — no annual contract is required.

**Q: What is a credit?**
Credits are how Nexus AI measures AI usage across the platform — chat,
Research Mode, Creative Studio, academic tools, code and more. Every plan
comes with a monthly credit allowance. You can top up at any time if you
need more before your credits reset.

**Q: What happens when I run out of credits?**
Your plan's core features remain active. AI-intensive actions (like advanced
model responses, image generation or Research Mode queries) will prompt you
to top up or upgrade. Top-ups start from $10 and are available instantly.

**Q: Can I use Research Mode on Premium?**
Yes. Premium includes 20 Research Mode queries per month. Ultimate includes
unlimited queries and longer reports (up to 20,000 words). Research Mode is
not available on the Free plan.

**Q: Is Nexus AI Academic the same as the AI Essay Writer?**
Yes — they are now one product. Nexus AI Academic is a unified research and
writing workspace that covers academic writing, essay drafting, citations,
plagiarism checking, peer review simulation, literature reviews and more.

**Q: Do file uploads count against my credits?**
Uploading a file does not cost credits. File operations — like summarising a
document or running File Chat — do use credits. All paid plans include
unlimited file uploads; the Free plan is limited to 5 files.

**Q: What is the minimum for a Teams plan?**
Teams plans require a minimum of 2 seats at $30/seat/month. There is no
annual contract — you pay month to month.

**Q: Can I add Meeting Notes to any plan?**
Yes. Meeting Notes is available as an add-on on Free, Premium, Ultimate and
Teams. On the Business plan, Meeting Notes is bundled in at no extra charge
(300 minutes/month included per account).

**Q: How do I get Education or Enterprise pricing?**
Contact our team directly. Education pricing is an annual institutional
licence. Enterprise is a custom contract. Both include dedicated onboarding and support.

**Q: Can I switch plans or cancel at any time?**
Monthly plans can be cancelled at any time. Annual plans can be cancelled and refunded on a pro-rated basis within 30 days. Contact support for assistance.

**Q: Is there a free trial of Premium?**
The Free plan lets you try Nexus AI with no commitment and no card required.
If you want to trial Premium features, contact us about an extended trial.
